A gorgeous place to enjoy your life.

A private yet accessible getaway, Montañez Residence is a hotel and events venue conveniently located in Guimba Nueva Ecija. Come to our peaceful bed and breakfast through our Mango tree-lined driveway. We offer spacious and relaxing rooms ideal for couples, families, and groups. Our onsite Long Table Restaurant and B Cafe serves hearty meals amidst aesthetic plants. For your leisure, we have a Children Playground and a swimming pool.
